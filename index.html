About

FlickZone is a Netflix Clone. It aims to emulate the modern streaming platform that allows users to browse and stream a curated selection of movies and TV shows. It is designed with a clean and intuitive interface and offers personalized playlists and seamless content discovery.

FlickZone connects users with a rich library of content, categorized by genre, ratings, and popularity.

The project was inspired by my interest in React. FlickZone presented a hands-on opportunity for learning this framework.
