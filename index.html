About

FlickZone is a modern streaming platform that allows users to browse and stream a curated selection of movies and TV shows. Designed with a clean and intuitive interface, it offers personalized playlists and seamless content discovery.

FlickZone connects users with a rich library of content, categorized by genre, ratings, and popularity. Whether you're in the mood for a blockbuster hit or an indie gem, FlickZone has something for everyone.

Inspired by my passion for React, FlickZone provided a hands-on opportunity to learn and implement this powerful framework.
