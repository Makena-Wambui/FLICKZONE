ABOUT

FlickZone was inspired by my passion for movies and technology. As a movie enthusiast, I wanted to create a platform where users could explore a curated selection of movies and TV shows, much like I enjoy discovering hidden gems on streaming platforms. The challenge of recreating a Netflix-like experience fueled my desire to improve my skills in web development.

This project has been a hands-on opportunity to deepen my understanding of front-end development. It is a Portfolio Project for Holberton School, where I have applied everything I’ve learned over the course of my studies. You can check out the full project on GitHub.

This project represents several weeks of dedication, where I explored different technologies and frameworks. It allowed me to combine my passion for web design with my technical skills.
